API Documentation

This is the specification for building SPLICE applications. The following list of arguments can be placed in code to retrieve their associated linguistic cues from text. Following the list of arguments is sample Python code for retrieving the XML embedded values.

Quantity

Cues based on frequencies

numChars

Total number of characters

numCharsMinusSpacesAndPunctuation

Total number of characters minus spaces and punctuation

numWords

Total number of words

numSentences

Total number of sentences

numPunctuation

Total number of punctuation marks

Parts of Speech

All linguistic cues in the Parts of Speech category are calculated using a part of speech tagger based on the Brown corpus.

numNouns

Total number of nouns

nounRatio

The ratio of nouns to the total number of words

numVerbs

Total number of verbs

verbRatio

The ratio of verbs to the total number of words

numAdjectives

Total number of adjectives

adjectiveRatio

The ratio of adjectives to the total number of words

numAdverbs

Total number of adverbs

adverbRatio

The ratio of adverbs to the total number of words

Immediacy

Cues that indicate immediacy

numPassiveVerbs

Total number of passive verbs

passiveVerbRatio

The ratio of passive voice verbs to the total number of verbs

Pronouns

Cues that count pronoun frequencies

firstPersonSingular

Total number of first person singular words

firstPersonSingularRatio

Total number of first person singular words divided by the total number of words

firstPersonPlural

Total number of first person plural words

firstPersonPluralRatio

Total number of first person plural words divided by the total number of words

secondPerson

Total number of second person words

secondPersonRatio

Total number of second person words divided by the total number of words

thirdPersonSingular

Total number of third person singluar words

thirdPersonSingularRatio

Total number of third person singluar words divided by the total number of words

thirdPersonPlural

Total number of third person plural words

thirdPersonPluralRatio

Total number of third person plural words divided by the total number of words

Positive Self Evaluation

Cues that relate to the positive self evaluation of a speaker.

iCanDoIt

The total number of "I can" phrases in the text.

doKnow

The total number of times phrases such as "I know", "I am sure", and "I'm positive" appear in the text.

posSelfImage

The total number of times someone positively portrays themself in the text (e.g., I am a great guy or I am happy).

Negative Self Evaluation

Cues that relate to the negative self evaluation of a speaker.

iCantDoIt

The total number of "I can't" phrases in the text.

dontKnow

The total number of times someone says they don't know something in the text.

negSelfImage

The total number of times someone portrays themself with a negative self image in the text (e.g., I am ugly or I'm stupid).

Influence

Cues that indicate the speaker is attempting to influence someone.

numImperatives

The total number of imperative statements in the text.

suggestionPhrases

The total number of suggestions in the text.

inflexibility

The total number of "inflexible" statements in the text.

contradict

The total number of times someone contradicts what has been stated.

totalDominance

Dominance language is a summation of inflexible language, imperative sentences, positive self evaluation, suggestions, and inflexible language.

dominanceRatio

The total number of sentences that include dominance language divided by the total number of sentences.

numAgreement

The total number of words that signify that the person accepts another's propositions.

agreementRatio

The total number of words that signify that the person accepts another's propositions divided by the total number of words.

Deference

Cues that indicate the speaker is accommodating outside influence.

askPermission

The total number of times permission is asked for in the text.

seekGuidance

The total number of times guidance is sought in the text.

totalSubmissiveness

The total number of instances of submissiveness language.

submissivenessRatio

The total number of sentences that include submissiveness language divided by the total number of sentences.

Whissel

Cues that count returns scores based on the Whissel Dictionary of Affect in Language

Imagery

Average imagery score.

Pleasantness

Average pleasantness score.

Activation

Average activation score.

Complexity

Cues that relate to text complexity

avgWordLength

Total number of characters minues spaces and punctuation divided by total number of words

avgSentenceLength

Total number of sentences divided by total number of words

numSyllables

Count of the total number of syllables

avgSyllablesPerWord

Total number of syllables divided by total number of words

numWordsWith3OrMoreSyllables

Total number of words with three or more syllables.

rateWordsWith3OrMoreSyllables

Total number of words with three or more syllables divided by total number of words.

numWordsWith6OrMoreChars

Total number of words with six or more characters.

rateWordsWith6OrMoreChars

Total number of words with six or more characters divided by total number of words.

numWordsWith7OrMoreChars

Total number of words with seven or more characters.

rateWordsWith7OrMoreChars

Total number of words with seven or more characters divided by total number of words.

LexicalDiversity

The length of a Set of all the words divided by the total number of words

complexityComposite

Number of three syllable words + number of commas + number of conjunctions + number of singular and mass nouns + number of plural nouns + average sentance length all divided by the number of words

Spoken Word

Cues that relate to a speaking or writing style

hedgeVerb

Total number of verbs that indicate hedging

hedgeConj

Total number of conjunctions that indicate hedging

hedgeAdj

Total number of adjectives that indicate hedging

hedgeModal

Total number of modal verbs that indicate hedging

hedgeAll

Total number of words that indicate hedging

numDisfluencies

The total number of disfluencies

disfluencyRatio

The rate at which disfluencies appear

numInterjections

Total number of interjections

interjectionRatio

The ratio of total interjections to the total number of words

numSpeculate

The number of speculative words

speculateRatio

The ratio of speculative words to total words

Expressivity

Total number of adverbs and adjectives divided by the total number of nouns and verbs

numIgnorance

Total number of phrases indicating ignorance

ignoranceRatio

Total number of phrases indicating ignorance divided by the total number of words

Pausality

Total number of sentences divided by the total number of punctuation marks

questionCount

Total number of question marks

questionRatio

Total number of question marks divided by the total number of sentences

hedgeUncertain

A combination of indefinites, demonstratives, hedgind words, uncertainty words, and the Loughran and McDonald (2011) uncertainty and weak modal dictionaries.

Tense

Counts of verbs of a specific tense

pastTense

Total number of verbs in the past tense

pastTenseRatio

Total number of verbs in the past tense divided by the total number of words

presentTense

Total number of verbs in the present tense

presentTenseRatio

Total number of verbs in the present tense divided by the total number of words

Sentiwordnet

Calculates positivity, negativity, and objectivity based on Sentiwordnet

SWNpositivity

Average positivity scored based on Sentiwordnet

SWNnegativity

Average negativity scored based on Sentiwordnet

SWNobjectivity

Average objectivity scored based on Sentiwordnet

Readability

Cues that return readability scores; readability scores should be calculated for text passages at least 100 words long

ARI

ARI reading index score

FRE

FRE reading index score

FKGL

FKGL reading index score

CLI

CLI reading index score

LWRF

LWRF reading index score

FOG

FOG reading index score

SMOG

SMOG reading index score

DALE

DALE reading index score

LIX

LIX reading index score

RIX

RIX reading index score

FRY

FRY reading index score

Example Post Request

Example written in Python 2.6

import urllib, urllib2

# Change the values of these variables
fileName = r'c:\test.txt'   # Put the name of the file you wish to analyze here
theCues = 'numChars numVerbs numSpeculate'             # Put list of cues here in a string seperated by spaces

# Don't change this section
# ======================================
url = 'http://splice.cmi.arizona.edu/SPLICE/post/postargs/'
theFile = open(fileName).read()
data = 'text=' + theFile + '&cues=' + theCues
req = urllib2.Request(url, data)
response = urllib2.urlopen(req)
# ======================================

# This is your variable that holds the results from SPLICE
the_data = response.read()
print the_data
